Google StreetView Converter v1.5.03

Use real world panoramas instead of boring skyboxes.

This tool converts StreetView URL's to cubemap.

How to use:

1. Find panorama (see many examples at http://www.mapcrunch.com/gallery or https://www.google.com/maps/views/streetview?gl=us)
2. Open panorama in new Google Maps window (see mapcrunch.jpg and googleStreetView.jpg)
3. Copy URL address (it looks like https://www.google.com/maps/@46.309863,6.076324,3a,75y,81.97h,120.37t/data=!3m5!1e1!3m3!1sKGf112kOlNIAAAQJODj8Mw!2e0!3e5)
4. Import StreetView Converter asset in Unity3D
5. Invoke Window/GoogleStreetViewConverter
6. Paste URL
7. Press Convert button

See screenplay at http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RLsdhn3_0Z8

Example scenes

1. downloadPreview - downloads panorama in realtime
- press Play to view panorama
- see parameters of Sphere object for Texture Tool script
2. preloadedPanorama - preloaded panorama in high quality
